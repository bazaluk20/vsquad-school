Stages of creating a 3D model:

Blocking

At this stage, the basic shape of the model is created, which helps determine the proportions and overall structure.

sculpting

The process of adding details to the basic shape of the model. As a result, we get “high poly” (a detailed model with a large number of polygons)

retopology 

Reworking the geometry of the model to optimize its polygonal mesh. This is an important process for creating a low poly model suitable for animation and rendering.

Baking

Baking involves transferring "high poly" to "low poly". This allows you to save all the small details, while reducing the number of polygons.

texturing:

The final stage, where the model receives its colors, materials and surface details.

Texturing is the process of creating materials and textures for 3D objects. This item is the last on the list, but far from the most important. The final look of the model depends on the texture, and what impression it will make on the user.

There are different styles and approaches when creating textures, including Hard-Painted, PRB, Stylized, Photorealistic, Pixel Art and Cel-Shading. On the course, we will consider a mixture of the first three, because they are the most vivid and in demand 🔥.
