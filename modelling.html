Stages of creating a 3D model:

Blocking

At this stage, the basic shape of the model is created, which helps determine the proportions and overall structure.

sculpting

The process of adding details to the basic shape of the model. As a result, we get a ‚Äúhigh poly‚Äù (a detailed model with a large number of polygons)

retopology¬†

Reworking the geometry of the model to optimize its polygonal mesh. This is an important process for creating a low poly model suitable for animation and rendering.

Baking

Baking involves transferring "high poly" to "low poly". This allows you to save all the small details, while reducing the number of polygons.

texturing:

The final stage, where the model receives its colors, materials and surface details.

Modeling is the process of creating three-dimensional objects, which are the basis for any 3D scene. How realistic and influential the final work will be depends on the accuracy and detail of the model.

There are many different modeling styles and techniques, including Hard-Surface, Organic, Low-Poly, and more. In the course, we will consider one of the most beautiful options, namely Stylized üî•
